What we check

Every idea gets scored across the dimensions that actually matter.

📊

Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)

Is the market big enough to support a real business? We estimate total addressable market and your realistic serviceable share.

⚔️

Competition Landscape

Who else is in this space? We map direct competitors, adjacent products, and identify your potential moat.

📣

Distribution Difficulty

Can you actually reach your users? We evaluate go-to-market channels, CAC estimates, and organic growth potential.

💰

Capital Requirements

How much money do you need to get to revenue? We break down development, marketing, and operational costs.

⏱️

Time to Market

How long until you can ship an MVP? We assess technical complexity and what it takes to get something in front of users.

🔁

Retention & Monetization

Will people come back and pay? We evaluate stickiness, pricing models, and long-term unit economics.

📐

Technical Feasibility

Can this actually be built with today's tech? We flag technical risks, regulatory hurdles, and infrastructure needs.

🎯

Problem-Solution Fit

Is this a real pain point or a nice-to-have? We evaluate how urgent and frequent the problem is for your target users.

📈

Timing & Trends

Is now the right time? We analyze market trends, emerging tailwinds, and whether the window of opportunity is open.
